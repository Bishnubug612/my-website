Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications can take 2-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.

What are your pricing models?

We offer both fixed-price and hourly billing options. After understanding your requirements, we'll recommend the best pricing model for your project.

Do you provide maintenance?

Yes! We offer ongoing maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally.

Are websites mobile-friendly?

Absolutely! All our websites are responsive and optimized for mobile devices, ensuring great user experience across all screen sizes.

Is SEO included?

We include basic SEO optimization in all projects. For comprehensive SEO strategies, we offer dedicated SEO services and packages.

What's your process?

We follow a structured process: Discovery, Planning, Design, Development, Testing, Launch, and ongoing support. You'll be involved every step of the way.
